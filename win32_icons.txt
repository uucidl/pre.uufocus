Application Icons
-----------------

Windows is aware of four different icon sizes:
- System Small ; SM_CXSMICON, SM_CYSMICON
- System Large ; SM_CXICON, SM_CYICON
- Shell Small
- Shell Large.

Developers are encouraged to include a minimum of the following
sizes and color depths in their icon resources:
- 16x16 16 colors
- 32x32 16 colors
- 48x48 256 colors
- 64x64
- 256x256
